Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Eris Markets, cryptocurrency data, and how to use our platform.

What is Eris Markets?

Eris Markets is a comprehensive cryptocurrency market data platform that provides real-time prices, analytics, and portfolio tracking tools for thousands of digital assets. Our mission is to make cryptocurrency data accessible and useful for everyone from beginners to experienced traders.

Is Eris Markets free to use?

Yes, our basic service is completely free and provides access to real-time prices, market data, and basic portfolio tracking. We also offer premium features for advanced users and traders who need more sophisticated tools and analytics.

How often are prices updated?

Our cryptocurrency prices are updated in real-time, with data refreshing every 30 seconds to ensure accuracy. We aggregate data from multiple reputable exchanges worldwide to provide the most accurate and timely information possible.

Where does your data come from?

We aggregate data from multiple reputable cryptocurrency exchanges worldwide to provide accurate and reliable market information. Our system processes millions of data points daily, ensuring that our users receive the most comprehensive and up-to-date market information available.

How accurate is your data?

We strive to provide the most accurate data possible by aggregating information from multiple sources and implementing rigorous quality control measures. However, cryptocurrency markets are highly volatile and data may occasionally have slight delays. We recommend always verifying critical information from multiple sources before making investment decisions.

Do you provide investment advice?

No, Eris Markets provides market data and analytical tools for informational purposes only. We do not offer investment advice, financial advice, or trading recommendations. All investment decisions should be based on your own research and consultation with qualified financial advisors.

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of our website. You'll need to provide an email address and create a password. After verifying your email, you'll have access to all basic features. For premium features, you can upgrade your account through the subscription page.

How do I set up price alerts?

To set up price alerts, first create an account and log in. Then navigate to any cryptocurrency page and click the "Set Alert" button. You can choose the price threshold and how you'd like to be notified (email, push notification, or both).

Can I track my portfolio on Eris Markets?

Yes, we offer comprehensive portfolio tracking tools. After creating an account, you can manually add your holdings or connect your exchange accounts via read-only API keys. Our system will then track your portfolio's performance, provide analytics, and send you personalized insights.

Do you have an API?

Yes, we offer a comprehensive API for developers. Our API provides access to real-time and historical market data, with various subscription tiers based on your needs. You can find documentation and sign up for API access on our Developer Portal.

What browsers are supported?

Eris Markets supports all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of your browser. Some features may not work correctly on older browsers or mobile browsers with limited capabilities.

Do you have a mobile app?

Yes, we have mobile apps for both iOS and Android devices. You can download them from the App Store and Google Play Store. Our mobile apps provide all the features of our web platform optimized for mobile devices, including price alerts, portfolio tracking, and real-time market data.
