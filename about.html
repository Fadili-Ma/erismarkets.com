About Eris Markets

We're on a mission to democratize access to cryptocurrency market data and empower investors with real-time insights and powerful analytics.

Our Story

Eris Markets was founded in 2020 by a team of blockchain enthusiasts and financial technology experts who recognized the need for a more accessible, transparent, and user-friendly platform for cryptocurrency market data.

In the rapidly evolving world of digital assets, we saw that investors and traders were struggling to find reliable, real-time information presented in an intuitive way. Traditional financial platforms were slow to adapt to the unique needs of crypto markets, while existing crypto platforms often prioritized complexity over usability.

We built Eris Markets to bridge this gap‚Äîcombining institutional-grade data with an interface that both beginners and experienced traders can appreciate. Today, we serve over 500,000 users worldwide who rely on our platform for accurate pricing, portfolio tracking, and market analysis.
